I'm Steve Roenkae

Thank you for checking out my abstract creations. I believe that art is in the process, not necessarily in the product, so please feel free to use my art in whatever way you feel drawn. These works are unnamed, save for numbers, and are open to interpretation.

I invite you to explore these works, see if they stir up any thoughts or feelings, and welcome any comment or story you may with to share via the contact form. Originals are available for purchase where available.
